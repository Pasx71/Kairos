Kairos Plugin Overview
Kairos is a comprehensive, highly-configurable survival enhancement plugin that introduces dynamic environments, grueling challenges, and immersive mechanics to completely overhaul the Minecraft survival experience.

Below is an outline of the core features included in the plugin.

🌦️ Dynamic Seasons
Experience an ever-changing world with a fully functional cycle of Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter.

Crop Growth & Greenhouses: Seasons dictate which crops can grow and how fast. Build greenhouses with adequate lighting to bypass seasonal restrictions.
Environmental Hazards: Seasons alter the world dynamically. Winter causes water to freeze and snow to layer, while Spring brings heavy rains that turn soil into slowness-inducing mud.
Fishing & Wildlife: Fishing yields and passive mob spawn rates fluctuate based on the harshness of the current season.
🌡️ Temperature & Hydration Systems
Players must actively manage their core body temperature and hydration levels to survive.

Temperature Scale: A dynamic 0-100 scale (where 50 is comfortable). Extreme cold causes freezing damage and slowness, while extreme heat results in nausea and heat strokes.
Environmental Influences: Temperature is affected by Biomes (Hot/Cold), Seasons, Time of Day, Rain, and nearby blocks (Campfires warm you up, Ice cools you down).
Armor Mechanics: Wearing Leather Armor provides vital insulation during Blizzards and Winter nights, while heavy armor may scorch the player in Hot biomes during Summer.
Thirst: Players must stay hydrated.
🌪️ Devastating Disasters
Nature fights back. Disasters can spawn naturally based on random timers, difficulty settings, or external integrations (like Player Karma/Luck).

Weather Disasters: Survive Extreme Winds, Blizzards, Sandstorms, Hurricanes, and Tornadoes. Disasters respect biomes (e.g., Sandstorms only in Deserts).
Geological Hazards: Beware of Earthquakes and underground Cave-ins that drop gravel and stone on unsuspecting miners.
Invasions & Purges: Defend against coordinated swarm events like Spider Infestations, Nether Purges, Bandit Raids, and the catastrophic Death Parade (a scheduled Winter invasion).
🧟 Custom Mobs
The nights are far more dangerous with custom entities integrated into the natural spawn pool.

New Threats: Encounter custom-scripted enemies like the Ancient Mummy, Blood Zombie, Ghoul, Lost Soul, Psyco, Scarecrow, Skeleton Knight, and Vampire.
Seasonal Shifts: The types of monsters that spawn change with the seasons (e.g., Husks dominate the Summer, while Strays and Polar Bears emerge in Winter).
🦠 Illness & Diseases
Players can contract crippling diseases from the environment or hostile mobs.

Contagious Spread: Diseases can spread from player to player via proximity.
Symptoms: Suffer through coughing fits, sneezing, slowness, and health deterioration until cured.
🌍 World Management & Integrations
Kairos is built to seamlessly blend into larger server ecosystems.

Biome Groups: Custom groupings (Hot, Cold, Wet, Oceanic, Magical) allow server owners to dictate exactly where certain seasons or disasters can occur.
Protection Integration: Full integration with WorldGuard and LandsAPI ensures that disasters won't destroy claimed land or spawn inside safe zones.
PlaceholderAPI: Create complex external systems! Use PAPI to link a player's "Karma" or "Luck" stat directly to their disaster frequency, or give specific races/classes innate temperature resistance.
⚙️ Fully Configurable
Everything from disaster frequency and mob health, to the exact block radius of a cave-in is defined intuitively through modular YAML configuration files. GUI menus exist for ingame management and hot-toggling event
